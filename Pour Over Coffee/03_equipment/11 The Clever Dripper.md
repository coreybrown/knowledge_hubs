# 🧠 The Clever Dripper

The **Clever Dripper** is the brilliant compromise between pour over and immersion. It looks like a chunky cone, but a **shut-off valve** in the base changes everything: set it on the counter and it holds water like a steeping pot; set it on your mug and the valve opens, draining the brew through a paper filter. You get **immersion's forgiveness with paper's clarity**, a combination that has made it a favorite for busy mornings and offices alike. See how it relates to other brewers in [[What Is Pour Over Coffee]].

| Flow speed | You choose: steep, then release |
| Cup style | Clean like pour over, sweet and round like immersion |

## How It Works: Steep, Then Drain

> [!example] The Clever method
> 1. Seat the [[Coffee Filters\|paper filter]] and add grounds. 2. Pour in *all* your water at once and stir. 3. Let it **steep** (typically 2–4 minutes). 4. Place the Clever on your mug; the valve opens and the brew drains through the paper.

Because the coffee **fully submerges** for the whole steep, extraction is even and the [[Pouring Technique|pour]] barely matters: there is no [[Bypass and Channeling|channeling]] to worry about. Then the paper filter catches the oils and fines on the way out, delivering the **clarity** an open immersion brewer like the [[French Press]] can't. The valve gives you precise control over [[Brew Time and Total Contact Time|total contact time]], the single most powerful variable here.

> [!tip] The most forgiving "pour over" cup
> Since the grounds steep rather than percolate, grind uniformity and pour skill matter far less. Dial flavor with **steep time** and [[The Brew Ratio|ratio]]. It's a superb brewer for beginners and a sneaky-good one for experts.

## Clever vs Other Immersion Brewers

It is paper-clean like a pour over but immersion-even like a [[French Press]] (which uses a metal mesh and leaves sediment). It overlaps heavily with the [[OXO Rapid Brewer]], a more recent steep-and-release design, and shares DNA with the inverted [[AeroPress]]. The [[Brew Method Comparison Table]] lines them all up.

## Recipes

Use the [[Clever Dripper Official Recipe]] as your baseline, then tune steep time to taste: longer for [[Sweetness and Body|body]], shorter for [[Acidity in Coffee|brightness]].
